BOOKING POLICIES

LATE ARRIVAL

Please arrive on time. A 15-minute grace period will be given, but after this, your appointment will be cancelled, and your deposit will not be refunded.

PAYMENT

All payments are required at the time of booking. Deposits are non-refundable unless the cancellation policy is followed.

CANCELLATION

A 24-hours notice is required prior to cancellation or rescheduling of an appointment.
